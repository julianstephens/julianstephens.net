Design Resources

I get asked all the time what resources I used to get better as a designer. After years of giving a different answer every time, I’ve finally compiled a “definitive” list of books, tools, and courses that I’ve used or been recommended during my design journey. I’ll continue to update this list as I find more or better resources, so be sure to bookmark and check back periodically for changes.

Books

  • The Design of Everyday Things by Donald Norman
  • Universal Principles of Design by William Lidwell
  • The Design Process by Karl Aspelund
  • 100 Things Every Designer Needs to Know About People by Susan Weinschenk
  • Solving Product Design Exercises: Questions and Answers by Artiom Dashinsky
  • Bigger Than This: How to turn any venture into an admired brand by Fabian Geyrhalter
  • The Brand Gap: How to Bridge the Distance Between Business Strategy and Design by Marty Neumeier
  • Creativity, Inc: Overcoming the Unseen Forces That Stand in the Way of True Inspiration by Ed Catmull
  • Steal Like an Artist: 10 Things Nobody Told You About Being Creative by Austin Kleon
  • Don’t Make Me Think: A Common Sense Approach to Web Usability by Steve Krug
  • The War of Art by Steven Pressfield
  • Pioneer of Swiss Graphic Design by Josef MÃ¼ller-Brockman
  • How To by Michael Bierut
  • Mismatch: How Inclusion Shapes Design by Kat Holmes
  • Ruined by Design: How Designers Destroyed the World, and What We Can Do to Fix It by Mike Monteiro
  • DESIGN{H}ERS: A Celebration of Women in Design Today by Victionary
  • Sprint (How to Solve Big Problems and Test New Ideas in Just Five Days) by Jake Knapp

Tools

  • Awesome Design Tools – a list of every kind of design tool you could ever want

Note: The list above is far more comprehensive than what I’ve compiled. Below you’ll find tools that I’ve used personally and would recommend to others. Consider these my MVPs.

  • Figma – a UX mockup and prototyping tool
  • Flaticon – free svg icons
  • Coolors – a super fast color scheme generator
  • Unsplash – free high-resolution photos
  • Pexels – royalty-free stock photos and videos
  • Affinity software – one-time $50 alternatives to Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign
  • HandBrake – a free tool to convert video from any format to a wide range of modern codecs
  • Origami Studio – a UX/UI prototyping tool
  • Invision – take designs from ideation to development with one tool
  • Threed.io – a free 3D mockup tool
  • Swach.io – a free color management tool
  • humaaans – free diverse illustrations of people
  • Lukasz Adam – more free illustrations
  • DrawKit - even more free illustrations
  • unDraw – you guessed it…free illustrations
  • CSS Gradient – all the gradients you could ever want
  • Laws of UX – a great site outlining different laws and principles of design
  • Notion – a modern knowledge management tool
